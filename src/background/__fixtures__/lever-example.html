About DesignCo

At DesignCo, we're building the next generation of design tools that empower creators worldwide. Our product is used by over 5 million designers every month.

What You'll Do

  • Lead the design of new product features from concept to launch
  • Create user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity mockups
  • Collaborate with engineers to implement pixel-perfect designs
  • Conduct user research and usability testing
  • Contribute to and maintain our design system

What We're Looking For

  • 3+ years of product design experience
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating user-centered design
  • Proficiency in Figma and other design tools
  • Experience with design systems and component libraries
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills

Compensation & Benefits

$120K - $160K base salary plus equity. Comprehensive health insurance, 401(k) matching, flexible work arrangements, and generous PTO.
