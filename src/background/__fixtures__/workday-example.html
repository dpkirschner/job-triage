Data Scientist

Boston, MA, United States
Posted 2 days ago

Job Summary

Global Analytics Corp is seeking an experienced Data Scientist to join our Machine Learning team. You will work on cutting-edge ML applications that impact millions of users globally.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and deploy machine learning models at scale
  • Analyze large datasets to extract actionable insights
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams on data-driven initiatives
  • Design and implement A/B tests and experiments
  • Present findings to stakeholders and leadership

Required Qualifications

  • PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field (or MS with 3+ years experience)
  • Strong programming skills in Python and SQL
  • Experience with TensorFlow, PyTorch, or similar ML frameworks
  • Deep understanding of statistical methods and machine learning algorithms
  • Excellent communication and data visualization skills

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with distributed computing (Spark, Hadoop)
  • Knowledge of deep learning and neural networks
  • Publications in top-tier ML conferences (NeurIPS, ICML, etc.)
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)

What We Offer

Competitive salary and equity package, comprehensive benefits including health/dental/vision insurance, 401(k) with company match, unlimited PTO, professional development budget, and a collaborative hybrid work environment.
